Hello, I am Nigel

Hi! I am currently a Third Year Computer Science Student at the National University of Singapore. Primarily interested in full stack web development and design.


About

Pursuing a major in Computer Science at the National University of Singapore. Experienced in front-end development and design prototyping, with knowledge in user interface, testing, and debugging processes. Proficient in a variety of technologies, including C#, React.js, and Python.

Beyond the realm of technology, I've also honed my interpersonal and leadership skills through my role as the head of a house within Tembusu College under the University Town College Program (UTCP). In this capacity, I led and nurtured a vibrant house community, fostering an environment of camaraderie and support. Through close collaboration with my peers, I've developed a keen sense of teamwork and effective communication.

Currently, I'm broadening my horizons as a software engineering intern at Synapxe, where I'm actively involved in real-world projects, refining my skills, and gaining invaluable industry experience.

Outside of my professional endeavors, I find joy in capturing moments through photography and embarking on exhilarating hikes. Exploring nature allows me to unwind, rejuvenate, and find inspiration in the beauty of the world around me.
